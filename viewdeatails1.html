Ahoy, Mateys!

Catalyst Crew's Bounty Quest was an epic Anime x Marvel-themed quiz adventure that tested the knowledge and instincts of true fans across three thrilling rounds. The event saw fierce competition from otaku champions across the region, with participants battling through increasingly challenging rounds to claim the ultimate bounty!

The Challenge Rounds

Round 1: East Blue Trials

The first elimination round featured 20 MCQs with emoji and silhouette clues. Participants had just 15 minutes to prove their basic knowledge across Anime and Marvel universes. Only those who followed @catalystcrewcreators on Instagram were eligible to advance, with one attempt allowed per participant.

Round 2: Sound of the Grand Line

Survivors faced sound-based clues, riddles, and speed-based logic challenges. This intense round tested quick thinking and deep knowledge under pressure, with participants needing to identify characters, scenes, and plot points from audio cues alone.

Round 3: Shattered Visions

The final round pushed contestants to decode distorted visuals, mysterious silhouettes, and hidden identities from both Anime and Marvel worlds. Only the sharpest minds could reconstruct the shattered visions to reveal the hidden truths beneath.
